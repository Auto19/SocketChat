UNZIP this Depository to your DESKTOP otherwise it will not work

1. Unzip the host.zip and client.zip files to their respective locations.
2. Simply click the Bullhorn_1.1SetupFile to begin the process of installing Bullhorn.
3. Click on the Bullhorn Icon on your desktop to run.
4. If you would like to host a bullhorn server go to "C:\PyJaric\Chat\Host\host files\host.exe"
5. click on it, you are now running a bullhorn server.
6. to connect to it go to your Bullhorn 1.1 and when asked for ip place 127.0.0.1 intead of your normal ip
7. Now you have bullhorn
